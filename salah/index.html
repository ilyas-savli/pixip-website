Features
Everything for your daily prayer
Designed to be calm, accurate and respectful — with thoughtful Arabic typography and full RTL support.
🕑

Accurate Prayer Times

Imsak, Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha — calculated for your city to the minute via the official Diyanet feed.

🧭

Live Qibla Compass

Real-time compass with rotating rose, cardinal letters and a haptic tap when you face the Kaaba.

📿

Digital Dhikr Counter

Tap to count Subhanallah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar — with progress ring, haptic per bead and undo.

📅

Hijri Calendar

Browse upcoming days with both Gregorian and Hijri dates, organized by Islamic month.

🔔

Prayer Reminders

Per-prayer reminders that fire on time, with adjustable offset (5, 10, 15 or 30 minutes before).

🌍

Multilingual

English, Türkçe, العربية — full right-to-left layout for Arabic users.

🌙

Light & Dark Modes

A peaceful parchment-and-teal palette by day, deep teal-and-gold at night.

🔒

Privacy by Design

No account, no profile, no upload. Your location is used only on this device to compute prayer times.

🛡️

Your location stays on your device

Salah uses your location only to compute local prayer times and the qibla bearing. It is never uploaded to a server, never associated with an account, and never shared. There is no account to create — the app is anonymous by design. Read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for the full details.
