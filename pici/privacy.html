Privacy Policy

Pici AI - Photo Beautifier & Style Transform

Effective Date: March 15, 2026
Last Updated: March 15, 2026

At Pici AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and authentication credentials
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details such as name and preferences
  • Photos and Images: Images you upload or capture within the app for AI processing, editing, and transformation
  • User Content: Any content you create, share, or store within the app

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features used, time spent in app, and crash reports
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics and user interaction data for app improvement, collected via Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA/GAID): With your permission (via the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS, or based on your device settings on Android), we may collect your advertising identifier to deliver personalized advertisements. You may withdraw this permission at any time in your device settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide AI-powered photo editing, beautification, and style transformation services
  • Account Management: Create and manage your user account and authenticate users
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Advertising: Display advertisements within the app. With your consent, we may show personalized ads based on your advertising identifier. Without consent, only non-personalized ads are shown
  • Customer Support: Respond to your requests, questions, and provide technical support
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics), and fraudulent activities
  • Communications: Send important updates, security alerts, and promotional content (with your consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. AI Processing and Your Photos

Important: Third-Party AI Service

To provide AI-powered photo beautification, style transformation, background removal, and other image editing features, your photos are sent to LightX Editor, operated by Andor AI Tools (lightxeditor.com). This is a third-party AI service.

What data is sent: The photo or image you select or capture within the app.

Who it is sent to: LightX Editor / Andor AI Tools, via their API at api.lightxeditor.com.

Purpose: Solely to perform the AI image processing operation you requested (e.g., beautification, background removal, AI filter, face swap, portrait enhancement, outfit try-on).

By using any AI feature in this app, you consent to your image being transmitted to LightX Editor for processing. Please review their privacy policy at lightxeditor.com/privacy-policy.

Additional details about photo processing:

  • Temporary Storage: Your image is temporarily uploaded to secure cloud storage (Amazon S3 via a pre-signed URL) solely to facilitate processing by the AI service
  • Automatic Deletion: Processed images are automatically deleted from our servers within 30 days unless you explicitly save them to your account
  • No Human Review: Your photos are processed automatically by AI; no human employees view your personal images
  • No AI Training: Your photos are not used to train any AI models

4. Advertising and Tracking

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). To show relevant ads, Google AdMob may collect and use your advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and other device information.

  • On iOS: Before collecting your advertising identifier, the app will show an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking for your permission. If you grant permission, personalized ads may be shown. If you decline, only non-personalized ads are shown and your IDFA is not used for ad targeting
  • On Android: You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's Google settings
  • What AdMob collects: Advertising identifier, IP address, device information, and app usage data for the purpose of serving and measuring ads
  • You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy and Google AdMob's policies at support.google.com/admob

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share your information only with the following service providers, and only as necessary to deliver the app's features:

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

  • LightX Editor / Andor AI Tools — AI image processing (see Section 3). Privacy policy: lightxeditor.com/privacy-policy
  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication (Firebase Auth), database (Firestore), cloud storage, app performance monitoring (Firebase Crashlytics), remote configuration (Firebase Remote Config), and analytics (Firebase Analytics). Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — In-app advertising. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — Optional authentication method. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Inc. — In-app purchases and subscriptions on iOS are processed by Apple via the App Store. Privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play (Google LLC) — In-app purchases and subscriptions on Android are processed by Google via Google Play. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — Temporary cloud storage used for image upload during AI processing. Privacy policy: aws.amazon.com/privacy

Each of these third-party providers has their own privacy policy and provides the same or equivalent level of data protection as required by applicable law.

5.2 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Secure Infrastructure: Use of secure cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase, AWS) with enterprise-grade security
  • Crash Monitoring: Firebase Crashlytics is used to detect and fix app crashes to maintain stability and security

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • Account Access: View and update your account information at any time within the app
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at pixip@pixip.tech
  • Opt-out of Tracking (iOS): You can change your ATT tracking permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads (Android): Go to Google Settings > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Opt-out of Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails using the link in the email

7.2 App Permissions

  • Camera: Access your camera to take photos for AI processing (optional; you can use the photo library instead)
  • Photo Library: Access your photo library to select images for AI processing
  • Tracking (iOS only): Access your advertising identifier for personalized ads (optional; you can decline the ATT prompt)
  • Internet: Required for AI processing, authentication, and app functionality

8. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Pici AI offers optional subscription plans (monthly and annual). Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment card information. We receive only a confirmation of the purchase and your subscription status from the respective platform. Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your App Store or Google Play account settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where our service providers (Google, AWS, LightX Editor) operate. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that our service providers implement appropriate safeguards.

  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
